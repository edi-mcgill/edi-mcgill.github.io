EPOD Discussions


EPOD (Education, Public Outreach, Equity, Diversity and Inclusion) Discussion is a weekly discussion group run by the MSI coordinator. EPOD is meant to give people a space in which to learn about and talk about equity, diversity, and inclusion and how these issues intersect with academia and education. The discussion is targeted at MSI members at all levels (undergraduate students, graduate students, postdoctoral fellows, and faculty), but we regularly welcome participants from any of our member departments even if they aren’t MSI members.

Winter 2022: every Wednesday, 2 pm EST
